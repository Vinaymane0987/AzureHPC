<h2 style="margin-top: 2pt; margin-left: 28.8pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; text-indent: -28.8pt; page-break-inside: avoid; page-break-after: avoid; font-size: 13pt;"><span style="font-family: 'roboto'; font-weight: normal; color: #2f5496;">2.1</span> <a name="_Toc85794197"></a><a name="_Toc85794524"></a><a name="_Toc85798828"></a><a name="_Toc88762755"></a><span style="font-family: 'roboto'; font-weight: normal; color: #2f5496;">Ansys</span> <span style="font-family: 'roboto'; font-weight: normal; color: #2f5496;">Rocky</span> <span style="font-family: 'roboto'; font-weight: normal; color: #2f5496;">Overview</span></h2>
<p style="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 8pt; text-indent: 36pt;"><a name="_Toc81498994"></a><a name="_Toc85794198"></a><a name="_Toc85794525"></a><a name="_Toc85798829"></a><span>Rocky is a powerful 3D Discrete Element Modelling (DEM) Particle Simulation Software that quickly and accurately simulates the flow behaviour of bulk materials with complex particle shapes and size distributions, for typical applications such as conveyor chutes, mills, mixers, and other materials handling equipment.</span></p>
<p style="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; text-indent: 36pt;"><span>Rocky is designed to solve engineering problems. Rocky is uniquely capable to models real particle shapes including any solids, 2D shells, and rigid and flexible fibres. The simulations are fast and accurate. With multi-graphics processing unit (GPU) solver technology, you can simulate the behaviour of different shaped and sized particles in many industrial applications.</span></p>
<p style="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span>Leading companies in the heavy equipment, off-road, mining, steelmaking, </span><span>agriculture equipment manufacturing and food processing </span><span>industries use </span><span>ROCKY</span><span> to understand and predict granular material behaviours, evaluate equipment performance, and optimize processes.</span></p>
<p style="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">Product Highlights</span></p>
<p style="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">Simulate Real-Scale Experiments</span></p>
<p style="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span>Rocky DEM&rsquo;s high-performance computing (HPC) abilities can simulate a large number of real-sized and real-shaped particles faster and with less memory usage.</span></p>
<p style="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">Model Realistic Materials</span></p>
<p style="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span>Modify a default shape, scan a real 3D object or custom design your own shape and import it. Then define the behaviour to be rigid or flexible, sticky or dry, breakable and more.</span></p>
<p style="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">Solve Unique Problems</span></p>
<p style="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span>Rocky </span><span>DEM&rsquo;s representation of application-specific particle shapes is becoming essential to the successful engineering of bulk materials handling equipment.</span></p>
