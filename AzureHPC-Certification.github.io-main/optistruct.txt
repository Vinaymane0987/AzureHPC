    <p style="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 8pt; line-height: 108%; font-size: 11pt;"><span style="font-family: 'Segoe UI';">This article briefly describes the steps for running </span><a style="text-decoration: none;" href="https://www.altair.com/optistruct"><span style="font-family: 'Segoe UI'; text-decoration: underline; color: #0000ff;">Altair Optistruct</span></a><span style="font-family: 'Segoe UI';"> application on a virtual machine (VM) that's deployed on Azure.     <p style="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 8pt; text-align: justify; line-height: 115%; font-size: 11pt; background-color: #ffffff;"><span style="font-family: 'Segoe UI';">OptiStruct&reg; from Altair is a contemporary structural analysis solver that tackles both linear and nonlinear simulation under static and dynamic loadings. This proven software is the most used solution for structural design and optimization in varied industries. Industrial structures have various parameters. OptiStruct&reg; helps engineers and designers optimize these parameters like strength, durability and NVH. This in turn helps the team develop innovative, lightweight, and structurally efficient designs.</span></p>
    <p style="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; text-indent: 36pt; text-align: justify; line-height: 150%; font-size: 11pt;"><span style="font-family: 'Segoe UI'; color: #333333; background-color: #ffffff;">OptiStruct provides comprehensive, accurate, and scalable solutions for statics and dynamics, vibrations, acoustics, fatigue, heat transfer, and multi-physics disciplines. It is used globally at industry-leading companies to drive design with optimization and validate structural performance. OptiStruct solves both linear and nonlinear problems using an enhanced proprietary version of NASTRAN and a modern proprietary nonlinear formulation developed and maintained by Altair.</span></p>
    <p style="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; text-indent: 36pt; text-align: justify; line-height: 150%; font-size: 11pt;"><span style="font-family: 'Segoe UI'; color: #333333; background-color: #ffffff;">OptiStruct topology optimization has driven the lightweight and structurally efficient designs of products and use every day. OptiStruct offers many other structural optimization methods and a broad range of essential manufacturing constraints for traditional processes, composites, and additive manufacturing.</span></p>
